Models map persistent business facts, ring-sale ledgers, named pigeon libraries, progressive categories, public information posts, registration detail publication state, global settings, and relationships. / 模型映射持久化业务事实、售环台账、命名足环库、递进类别、公开信息发布、报名明细发布状态、全局设置与关系。
They expose lifecycle helpers and cache invalidation only when the rule belongs to the aggregate. / 只有属于聚合本身的规则才暴露生命周期辅助方法与缓存失效。
