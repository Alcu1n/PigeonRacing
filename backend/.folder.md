Laravel is the trusted API, transaction, admin, import, export, and cache boundary. / Laravel 是可信 API、事务、后台、导入导出与缓存边界。
It accepts normalized member entries and persists validated registration snapshots. / 它接收标准化会员报名注并保存校验后的报名快照。
Frontend totals and pigeon ownership are never trusted without backend validation. / 前端金额与足环归属未经后端校验绝不可信。
